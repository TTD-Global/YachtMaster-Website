Effective Date:02.02.2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to Yacht Master! This Privacy Notice explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal information when you visit our website. By using our website, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Notice.

Non-Personal Information
